This is only an alternative way of playing "War":

  • 2 Players
  • standard 52-card deck
  • the cards have the normal rank from highest to lowest (ace, king, queen, jack, and then 10 through 2)
  • each player turns up a card at the same time
  • whoever turns over the highest card scores the card value
  • if the two cards played are of equal value, then there is a "war" and no one scores
  • the game ends when there are no more remaining cards
  • the player with the highest score wins
